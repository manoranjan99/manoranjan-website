Student Diary

An Application for College Students to maintain class schedule and track attendance.

Google Playstore Link: Link to Play Store
This is an application for college students to maintain their schedules and keep track of their attendance. Some of the features of this app include :
  • • Dates of leave can be checked easily.
  • • Attendance percentage can be set manually, as per the requirement.
  • • Different timetables can be added and arranged as required.
  • • Attendance status can be updated daily to keep accurate track of percentages.
  • • Any missed class attendance can be added to the present attendance without any hassle.
  • • Can be shared with friends and faculty through Whatsapp.
This app is a personal undertaking that was pursued in order to make college life simple for the people around me. I collaborated with one another guy from my college on this project. We worked extensively on this project for around 2 months, in the summer of 2019. To come up with ideas for the aesthetics of the app, I took inspiration from a lot of other simple yet successful apps out there. I used Adobe Photoshop, illustrator, and XD to complete the aesthetics and looks of the app.
Interface design: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Adobe Xd.
Software Development Environment: Android Studio.
