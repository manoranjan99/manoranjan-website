HealthyKID Healthcare Application

Developed an enterprise app for Trawello Healthcare Services Pvt. Ltd. named 'HealthyKID Parent'.

App features:
  • • Authenticates users using OTP with their personal number (using firebase service)
  • • Regular health updates and screening data will be updated in the app.
  • • AI-based Growth chart for height, weight, and BMI with suggestions.
Play Store Link - Early Access ( Only for parents who have taken the subscription can log in)
Featured on DD channel (youtube link)

The main goal/agenda of the team is to make regular health screening easily available for every child to track their health every year. This will eventually reduce major health issues at starting stage.

About Trawello Healthcare
Trawello is a health-tech start-up working at the confluence of healthcare, education, and technology. Founded by alumni of NIT Trichy and IIM, Trawello is the winner of the prestigious Ten Minute Million award from IIT Bombay. They provide innovative solutions to educational institutions in the domain of healthcare, hygiene, health awareness, and safety.
Trawello provides a tech-based health screening service, called "HealthyKID", primarily focused on campuses. The emphasis is on both physical and mental wellness. They also offer HealthyCampus solutions to make the campus safe and hygienic.
My role as an application developer involves, designing and developing an entire application that authenticates the user via OTP service and populates the screening data on the interface.
Design
I worked on the front end; I needed to design and make all interfaces. I joined with my co-founder- Mr. Dusmant, drawing wireframes on paper first with some ideas and color. At last, we came up with a form-based screen, then we used Adobe Xd to visualize and prototype it. We chose green to get the feel of nature and trust since it is a health app.
Development
I used android studio to develop the entire application. XML for frontend, Java for working and consuming APIs.
I need to connect to three backend services.
  • • OTP service to authenticate the user.
  • • Cloud database to populate the data.
  • • Custom API service to load the growth chart.
Added Features
  • • Notification alert.
  • • Default picture.
  • • Slides of child photos at different screening dates.
  • • Color codes to denote the health condition.
Testing
Each feature has been completed as each module, and it has been tested and merged. Unit testing was done for each class and service. Overall testing has been done at last.
The entire application was developed in a period of six months. The initial stage of planning, design, and development was undertaken in December 2019. Features and testing phase from March to July 2020.
Used Photoshop, Illustrator to design the posters and other app assets.
