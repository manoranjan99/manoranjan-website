Service Offerings
Comprehensive Intelligence Report
Stay up-to-date with all topics of interest through daily/weekly email updates consolidating all relevant articles and providing concise unified insights, and also receive alerts on potential early warnings to allow users to stay ahead of the market. We provide concise newsletters telling you what’s important corresponding to the subjects you are interested in.

Topic Insights
Extensively monitor topics over multiple mediums (i.e. >20K news sources, twitter, reddit, job reviews, financial statements, etc.), and obtain unified summaries, sentiment analysis, surface potential impact events, ask specific questions, and much more. We ingest all the important information in real time, and read through it for you, and just tell you what’s important.
Learn more about Topic Insights
Comparison Suite
Compare different topics of interest based on both sentiment and specific attributes. See trend analysis for topics of interest, i.e. if interested in semiconductors, compare the sentiment of NVIDIA vs AMD, and also perform a textual aspect based comparison, i.e. ask how NVIDIA and AMD differ based on the aspect GPU.
Learn more about Comparison Suite
Document Insights
Upload your custom documents and media, i.e. pdfs, docs, audio, video, etc., and obtain summarizations catered to your preferences, i.e. lengths, certain aspects, etc.. Also, can combine data, i.e. combine a research paper with a youtube video, etc. and obtain unified Photon Insights, and ask questions across documents.
Learn more about Document Insights
Chrome Browser Extension
With just the click of a button, obtain a “Photon” (a summarization plus sentiment analysis) of any webpage. When surfing the internet and doing research, you will never have to go through long articles, emails, etc. again - instead just “Photon” them and obtain salient insights within seconds, saving many hours of reading!

Why Photon Insights?
It is burdensome to process large sets of information, but it doesn’t have to be. We allow you to quickly uncover and track insights from various sources – including news articles, social media, financials, audio/video data, and more – we spent a lot of time developing our proprietary AI and ML technologies so you should save your time! Photon Insights provides the end-user with salient insights by leveraging the power of generative AI, but unlike many other companies leveraging LLMs, we also let you map back to the original sources for full context. With our numerous product offerings and simple design, we will allow you to efficiently surface the relevant information you need for decision making.
Instant Insights
We are constantly scouring the web and generating up-to-date insights, so that users can stay on top of all their relevant topics. Additionally, our chatbot is in realtime, and our DocuInsights are completed within minutes - with Photon, we encourage you to be impatient!
Quality Information
Photon allows the user the ability to filter between sources of relevance (or upload their custom data) and effectively control the input from which we perform machine learning and dimensionality reduction - Photon provides insights only from the appropriate data.
Time Saving
Rather than reading through 1000s of articles and media, we do it for you, saving many hours every day for anyone who has to perform research. Photon strives to make businesses hyper-efficient, allowing them to more quickly and effectively make decisions.
