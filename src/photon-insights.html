Photon Insights - Founding Software Engineer and Product Designer

Jun 10, 2022, I joined a US-based startup named 'Photon Insights' as Founding Software Engineer and Designer. Photon Insights is an AI startup focused on insight generation to produce concise information from a large set of data. This will be useful for students, teachers, and business analysts to surface quality information within a short period of time.

The main product is insight generation and sentiment analysis from different news articles and social media.

This has many sub-products which produce real-time insights of any webpage content, document files and URL. I was joined as the fourth person in this startup as a frontend engineer and designer.

My Journey in the Startup.
I joined as a fourth member of the startup. I contributed to ideation, and concept creation from the demo version to complete design and development of version 1 of all products.
Early Stage
Ideated with the team, designed and developed version 1 of all products, reports templates, and company website from scratch.
  • • Designed version 1 logo, UI Screens, and report templates using Adobe xd, and Illustrator.
  • • Developed company website with Bootstrap.
  • • Constant iteration of the product with new ideas, creating new web pages and resolving all bugs.
